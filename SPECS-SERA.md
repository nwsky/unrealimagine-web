## Works 详情页差异化内容

### fractured-sunset
- **Tagline：** A fractured timeline. A single truth.
- **longDescription：** Fractured Sunset follows a woman reconstructing the last 72 hours before her sister's disappearance — told in reverse, each scene a different version of the same moment. A study in unreliable memory and emotional precision.
- **Format：** 6-episode micro-drama, ~8 min/episode
- **Positioning：** Emotional thriller. Designed for premium streaming.
- **Status：** In active development. Pilot script complete.
- **Tone：** Slow-burn. Intimate. Disorienting in a controlled way.

---

### second-signal
- **Tagline：** The message came twice. No one believed the first one.
- **longDescription：** Second Signal is a near-future thriller about a communications engineer who intercepts a distress signal — and realizes she sent it. A contained, high-concept story built around a single location and a tight cast.
- **Format：** 4-episode micro-drama, ~10 min/episode
- **Positioning：** Sci-fi thriller. Pitch-ready for co-production.
- **Status：** Treatment and visual lookbook complete. Seeking development partners.
- **Tone：** Tense. Cerebral. Visually stark.

---

### after-the-scene
- **Tagline：** What happens when the cameras stop.
- **longDescription：** After the Scene is a character study set inside a struggling regional film festival. Over four days, five people orbit the same question: why do they still make things no one watches? A story about creative obsession and quiet failure.
- **Format：** 5-episode micro-drama, ~12 min/episode
- **Positioning：** Drama. Festival-circuit and branded content crossover potential.
- **Status：** First draft complete. In refinement.
- **Tone：** Dry. Honest. Quietly funny.
